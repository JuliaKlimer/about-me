Biography

I was born on September 27 in Grodno, Belarus. At the age of 7, I went to primary school. After 4th grade I entered Gymnasium 2, Grodno. In 2014, my family moved to Ostrovets. So I graduated Gymnasium 1, Ostrovets in 2019. After successful passing the exams I entered Yanka Kupala State University, Faculty of Mathematics and Computer Science, Information Resources Management where I am studying now on the 2nd year.
